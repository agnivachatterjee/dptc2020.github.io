About us

4th International Conference on Electronics, Materials Engineering and Nano-Technology (IEMENTech 2020) will be organized by Dept. of ECE, Institute of Engineering & Management, Kolkata at IEM Gurukul Campus, Saltlake, Kolkata, India during 2nd to 4th October,2020. The scientific deliberations in the form of invited talks and contributory papers will cover diversified topics on Electronics, Materials Engineering, Nano Science and Technology.This will be an ONLINE conference.The IEEE Conference record no. of IEMENTech 2020 is #51367.

Contributory and accepted papers presented in the technical sessions will be sent to IEEE and possibly will be published in Conference Compendium and IEEE Digital Explorer (Scopus Indexed).

IEMENTech 2020 is listed in the IEEE conference listing HERE.
